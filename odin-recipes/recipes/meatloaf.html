Meatloaf

Go back to the homepage

This is a classic meatloaf recipe that is easy to make and delicious.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Eggs
  • Onion
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix ground beef, breadcrumbs, eggs, chopped onion, and spices.
  3. Shape the mixture into a loaf and place it in a baking dish.
  4. Bake for 1 hour or until cooked through.
  5. Let it rest for 10 minutes before slicing.
  6. Serve with mashed potatoes and gravy.
