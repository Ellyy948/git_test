Makhani Chicken

Go back to the homepage

This is a delicious Indian dish made with chicken, butter, and spices.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Butter
  • Tomato
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken.
  2. Cook the chicken in butter.
  3. Add spices and tomato.
  4. Simmer until cooked through.
